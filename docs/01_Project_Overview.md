# 2. Project Overview

The Car Dealership Inventory Management System is a role-based web application designed to streamline dealership inventory operations. The system enables administrators to manage vehicle inventory efficiently while allowing employees to quickly search available vehicles and complete sales.

Unlike a simple sales application, the primary focus of this project is inventory management. Vehicle sales, restocking, and inventory updates are treated as business operations performed on the inventory.

The application aims to improve inventory accuracy, reduce manual work, simplify daily dealership operations, and provide a better customer experience through faster vehicle search and efficient inventory management.

---

# 3. Business Understanding

A car dealership is an inventory-driven business whose primary responsibility is to acquire, maintain, manage, and sell vehicles.

The software focuses on managing the complete lifecycle of vehicles inside the dealership, from arrival and inventory registration to customer purchase and inventory updates.

The system is designed to replace manual inventory tracking methods such as paper records or spreadsheets with a centralized digital solution.

# 7. Vehicle Management Strategy

Each physical vehicle is stored as an individual inventory record.

Instead of maintaining stock quantities, every vehicle has its own unique identity (for example, VIN/Chassis Number).

Inventory summaries are generated by grouping available vehicles by model.

When a vehicle is sold, its status changes rather than reducing a quantity counter.

This approach better reflects how real-world dealerships manage vehicle inventory while remaining practical for the scope of this project.

---

# 8. Business Problems

The current manual workflow introduces several operational challenges:

- Slow vehicle searching
- Inaccurate inventory records
- Risk of duplicate vehicle sales
- Limited visibility into inventory status
- Manual record management
- Inefficient inventory restocking
- Lack of role-based access control

---

# 9. Project Objective

To develop a secure, role-based dealership inventory management system that enables administrators to efficiently manage vehicle inventory while allowing employees to quickly search and sell vehicles.

The system aims to maintain accurate inventory, simplify restocking, reduce operational errors, improve customer service, and support efficient dealership operations.
